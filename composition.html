Featured Project

.Pierrot. — Artist Project

.Pierrot. is a musical project that revolves around the discovery of dreams through the eyes of a sad clown travelling in oniric landscapes. By chasing the absurd and the bizarre, the musical practice is based on intricate sound design and peculiar harmonies.

The project started as an introspection but developed towards a research into exploring and discovering musical formulas that explain feelings, emotions, and the secretive internal world that guide us. Using music as an alchemist would.

Listen on Spotify: Drops of Blue · Greengrass & Purplefog

Available on all major streaming platforms: Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, Bandcamp, and more. Follow .Pierrot. for upcoming releases and sonic explorations.
