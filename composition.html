Composition

.Pierrot. — Artist Project

A musical project exploring the discovery of dreams through the eyes of a melancholic traveller navigating surreal landscapes. By embracing the absurd and bizarre, the practice is grounded in intricate sound design and unconventional harmonies.

What began as personal introspection evolved into research on musical formulas that articulate emotions and the secretive internal world guiding us. Using music as an alchemist would.
